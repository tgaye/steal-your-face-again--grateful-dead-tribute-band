About The Band

Keeping the Dead Alive

Steal Your Face Again is Colorado's premier Grateful Dead tribute band, formed in 2022 by a group of lifelong Deadheads who wanted to share their love for the music with the world.

We're not just a cover band - we're a celebration of the Grateful Dead's legacy, bringing the spirit of the Dead to new generations while staying true to the improvisational magic that made the original band so special.

Our members have played together in various Colorado bands for over a decade, and our chemistry on stage creates that same magical energy that Deadheads crave.

Join us on tour and experience the music that never stopped!
